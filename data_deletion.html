Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with our application, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. Select Apps and Websites from the sidebar menu.
  4. Find our app and click Remove.

Once you remove the app, we will no longer have access to your data.
